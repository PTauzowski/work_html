The venue / Warsaw city

The venue

The conference venue is located in central Warsaw near the Old Town, where there are also many restaurants, cafés, and shops. The conference venue is easily accessible by public transport and is directly connected by bus with Warsaw Airport (a distance of about 10 km).

The Conference will take place in The Old Library of Warsaw University, Krakowskie Przedmieście Street 26/28.

The venue is easily accessible by public transport. Frederic Chopin Airport is situated only 10 kilometers from the conference venue. There is direct connection from the airport to downtown and the conference site.
