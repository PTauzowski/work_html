Dear Collegues,

We would like to invite you to participate in the 41st Solid Mechanics Conference which will be held on August 27-31, 2018 in Warsaw, Poland. The Solid Mechanics Conferences have been organized by the Institute of Fundamental Technological Research since 1953. The conferences have maintained high scientific standard and served as a forum for exchange of ideas and research information.

We look forward to receiving your abstracts and welcoming you to Warsaw.

Yours Sincerely,
