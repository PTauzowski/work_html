Dear Collegues,

We would like to invite you to participate in the 41st Solid Mechanics Conference, which will be held in Warsaw on August 27-31, 2018.

The Solid Mechanics Conferences are a series of biannual international conferences, which have been organized by the Institute of Fundamental Technological Research of the Polish Academy of Sciences (IPPT PAN) since 1953. They aim at maintaining high scientific standards and providing a friendly atmosphere, facilitating discussions of research topics on solid mechanics.

We are looking forward to receiving your abstract and meeting you in Warsaw.

Yours Sincerely,
