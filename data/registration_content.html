Registration

The on-line registration will begin on January 2018.

During the registration process you will create a personal account which enables you to log in, submit an abstract, book a hotel and pay the Conference Fee by a credit card.

The on-line registration will be open soon.

Reminder: Each paid registrant at SOLMECH 2016 will be limited to one presentation. Participants may be an author of multiple abstracts, but may be the presenting author of only one abstract. When a paid registration is cancelled before August 1st, 2018 then 50% of the registration fee will be reimbursed. After this date, the registration fee cannot be reimbursed.

The registration fees covers:

  • Welcome Reception, lunches and coffee breaks, Conference Gala Dinner,
  • Participation in all sessions and the electronic conference materials (for fully registered participants and young scientists only)

Other social events covered by the conference fee will be announced later.
